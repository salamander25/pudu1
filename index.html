Questions You Might Be Asking

Are robots going to replace my staff?

No. The best deployments use robots to support staff, not replace them. Think of them as tireless runners that free your best people to do what humans do best.

How much do these robots cost?

Pricing depends on the model, number of robots, and your deployment. On your discovery call we’ll give you ballpark numbers, typical ROI timelines, and options that respect your budget.

What if my space is small or oddly shaped?

That’s exactly what the discovery call is for. Some spaces aren’t a fit; others just need a smart route layout. We’ll be transparent either way.

How long to go from “interested” to “live robots”?

Typical timelines range from a few weeks to a couple of months, depending on layout, shipping, and training.
